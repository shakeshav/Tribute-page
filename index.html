Shushant Singh Rajput


Great Indian Actor and Engineer who played a leading role in the indian cinema for making the bollywood on top list.

About the Legend

☛ SSR in full Sushant Singh Rajput, was born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, India..

☛ He worked as a indian actor from 2013 to 2020.

☛ Sushant Singh Rajput pursued a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Delhi College of Engineering (now known as Delhi Technological University).

☛ In 2008, he moved to the Indian cinema, where he started his carrier as tv serial.

☛ Later in 2013, Shushant planned to move from tv serial for bigger role in cinema and he started his carrier as a film actor.

☛ Among those successes in his film carrier,he become India’s first actor to claim the 7th rank in all india engineering .

☛ He was academically gifted, securing a high rank in the AIEEE and excelling in his studies.

☛ Sushant had a keen interest in astronomy and space science. He owned a Meade LX-600 telescope and often spent time stargazing. He was also known to be an avid reader of scientific literature.

☛ He was passionate about various sports, including cricket. His role as a cricketer in the film "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" showcased his sporting skills.
